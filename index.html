About Me

I'm Abstract, a certified keyboard warrior and digital introvert. My natural habitat is a dark room lit by multiple monitors, where I'm either crafting code or yelling at pixels. My social skills are mostly limited to in-game emotes and passive-aggressive pings, but my coffee-to-code ratio is impeccably optimized. I'm less of a "people person" and more of a "my character's-inventory-is-perfect person," and honestly, I wouldn't have it any other way.
